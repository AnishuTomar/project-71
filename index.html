What's new

  • Methodist high School computer labs

    The Methodist High School also has a computer department to support the educational needs of the school and to enhance the curriculum. Today, the school has three fully furnished, air-conditioned computer labs equipped with all the latest equipment. The senior lab has 40 latest computer systems with four printers. The junior and the Primary labs have 20 computers each with LCD Projectors.

  • Libraries

    The Methodist High School Libraries are a well stocked, up to date treasure of knowledge. We have two libraries in the school – the Primary and the Senior School Library. The Library is dedicated to Miss. Ethel Whiting, former Principal of Methodist High School, who rendered her services faithfully for 13 years. All the books in the Library are arranged as per Dewey Decimal Classification Scheme. The books are easily accessible by the students and staff. Every student is entitled to borrow two books at a time.
